# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Adaptiva AI serves business and AI leaders, Microsoft 365 programme owners, teams, and individuals navigating AI adoption. Organisational decision-makers are the primary entry point: they need to evaluate readiness, shape a practical roadmap, build confidence, and turn Copilot or broader AI investment into measurable outcomes. Practitioners and teams are the people who ultimately need useful skills, relevant guidance, and support that fits how they work.

## Product Purpose

The website establishes Adaptiva AI as a trusted partner for people-centred AI strategy, Copilot adoption and enablement, and AI-powered digital content creation. It should turn uncertainty into a clear next step, demonstrate that support is tailored to each organisation's maturity and goals, and convert qualified interest into consultations and complimentary sessions.

## Brand Personality

Credible, human, empowering. The voice should pair expert confidence with warmth and plain language, helping visitors feel capable rather than overwhelmed.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a generic AI startup. Avoid neon technology spectacle, interchangeable SaaS layouts, abstract futurism without substance, and claims that make AI feel detached from real people or work.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with human and organisational outcomes, not technology for its own sake.
2. Make expertise tangible through specific services, evidence, and useful examples.
3. Tailor before prescribing; reflect different maturity levels, roles, and ambitions.
4. Show practical value and a clear next step on every key journey.
5. Earn trust through clarity, consistency, and credible restraint.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Preserve and improve the site's current best-effort accessibility. Maintain semantic structure, keyboard access, visible focus states, readable contrast, responsive layouts, descriptive alternatives for meaningful media, and reduced-motion support where motion is introduced.
