About

Helping organisations adopt AI with clarity and confidence.

Adaptiva AI is led by Shabnam Sarjami, an award-winning AI professional whose work spans analytics, strategy, modern workplace transformation, and people-centred adoption.

A mission to inspire and guide every AI journey.

Shabnam Sarjami is an award-winning AI professional with a mission to inspire and guide organizations and individuals on their AI journey. Her career has been driven by a passion for helping organizations make better decisions through data, technology, and innovation. Starting at the intersection of analytics and strategy, she became an early adopter of Microsoft Power BI, spending over a decade transforming complex data into meaningful, visually engaging, and thoughtfully designed dashboards. As AI began reshaping the workplace, her focus naturally evolved toward AI enablement and adoption.

Today, she helps individuals and organizations unlock value from Microsoft 365 Copilot, AI agents, and modern workplace technologies. Her work spans Copilot adoption, AI coaching, digital learning, and creative content creation, empowering people to build confidence, capability, and meaningful outcomes with AI.

Signature strengths

  • Executive advisory with a clear view of business value and sponsorship.
  • Microsoft ecosystem expertise across Power BI, Copilot, and modern work.
  • Adoption-first delivery that accounts for confidence, behaviours, and change.
