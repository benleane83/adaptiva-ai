About

Helping organisations adopt AI with clarity and confidence.

Adaptiva AI is led by Shabnam Sarjami, an award-winning AI professional whose work spans analytics, strategy, modern workplace transformation, and people-centred adoption.

A career built at the intersection of data, technology, and change.

Shabnam’s career has been driven by a passion for helping organisations make better decisions through data, technology, and innovation. Starting at the intersection of analytics and strategy, she became an early adopter of Microsoft Power BI and spent more than a decade turning complex data into meaningful insights.

As AI reshaped the workplace, her focus evolved into AI enablement and adoption. Today, she helps organisations unlock value from Microsoft 365 Copilot, AI agents, and modern workplace technologies through executive advisory, readiness assessments, adoption strategies, and change management.

She is particularly passionate about the human side of AI: encouraging curiosity, building confidence, and helping people discover new ways of working through practical, innovative solutions.

Signature strengths

  • Executive advisory with a clear view of business value and sponsorship.
  • Microsoft ecosystem expertise across Power BI, Copilot, and modern work.
  • Adoption-first delivery that accounts for confidence, behaviours, and change.
